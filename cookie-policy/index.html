Cookie Policy

This page explains the limited browser storage that may be used when you visit the SteadyDesk IT website.

Last updated: July 21, 2026

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small data files that a website may place in a browser. Similar technologies can remember preferences, maintain security, or measure how a site is used.

2. Cookies used by this site

We design this website to work without advertising cookies. Essential hosting or security technology may use short-lived identifiers needed to deliver pages or prevent abuse.

3. Analytics and advertising

We do not currently use behavior-based advertising cookies on this website.

4. Managing browser storage

You can remove or block cookies using your browser settings. Blocking essential storage may affect security checks or form operation.

5. Changes and questions

Questions may be sent to support@steadydeskit.com.
