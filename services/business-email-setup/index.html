From domain to daily inbox

Professional email that is easier to trust, use, and hand over.

Business email touches customers, invoices, password recovery, and internal decisions. We build the setup around company ownership and reliable delivery so it does not depend on a former employee, a personal address, or an undocumented domain account.

Domain and delivery records

We confirm domain control and configure the records used to route mail and help receiving systems recognize legitimate messages.

Addresses that match the work

Individual mailboxes, aliases, and shared addresses such as billing or support are separated according to who needs to send, receive, and retain mail.

Safer sign-in and recovery

Multi-factor authentication, recovery contacts, admin roles, and device access are set up without making one person the single point of failure.

A controlled cutover

What we verify before changing business email.

Email changes can affect every employee and every customer conversation. We document the existing setup, protect important history, and choose a transition window with clear validation steps.

Domain ownership

Registrar access, DNS control, renewal responsibility, and company recovery contacts are confirmed before records are edited.

Mailbox map

Current users, shared addresses, forwarding rules, aliases, and inactive accounts are reviewed before new licenses are assigned.

Mail and calendar history

We identify which messages, folders, contacts, calendars, and delegated access need to move and how completion will be checked.

Delivery validation

Inbound, outbound, reply, forwarding, mobile, and external-delivery tests confirm that the new setup works beyond a single inbox.

Common questions

Will we lose old email?+

A planned migration usually preserves existing mail. We confirm what can be moved and how long the transition should take before making changes.

Can you create addresses like billing@ or support@?+

Yes. We can create shared mailboxes, groups, or aliases and define who should receive and respond to them.
